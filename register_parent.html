    PARENT AND STUDENT REGISTRATION FORM- 1 TO 1 HOME TUITION

    Dear Parent/ Student,
    Please fill in this form if you are finding a tutor for your child, or for yourself as the student. After you have registered a tuition account with us, you will be able to post a tuition job and you will be able to view the individual tutors' profiles and invite those tutors that you are interested in to apply for the tuition job you have posted. Alternatively, you may also wait for our tuition coordinators to call, to present a suitable tutor's profile to you.

    Our 3-step (1-2-3) procedure to getting a home tuition teacher from Star Zest Home Tuition:

    1) Register an account by filling in your particulars below
    2) Post a Tuition Job (it will direct you to our homepage to post a tuition job)
    3) View individual Tutor Profiles and key in your Job ID to invite tutors to apply to teach your child or simply wait for tutors to apply and our tuition consultants will call you to present their profile for your consideration


    Please feel free to call us at 63845607 if you encounter any problems posting your tuition job. We will be glad to be of service to you!
