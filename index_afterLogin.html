    About Star Zest Tuition Agency

    Star Zest Tuition Agency was established by Mr Law Hanwei.He started providing 1 to 1 tuition at home to primary school students after completing his O levels and in his school days as a polytechnic student.

    His dream was to set up a tuition agency whereby all tutors are being structurally interviewed and screened. He believes that conducting a business with honesty and integrity is the key to success.Up till today, we have a total of more than 40,000 registered tutors and have served more than 30,000 students in Singapore.

    Being a leader and role model in home tuition agency services industry in Singapore, we were interviewed by the Straits Times Newspaper on 28 Aug 2010 and The New Paper in 2011. To view the exclusive interview article, visit http://www.starzesttuitionagency.com.sg
